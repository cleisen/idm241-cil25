Microinteraction:
A little column extra info. Aaand just a little bit more
How it Works:

Triggers

  • Hover on & off image of TV show

  • Hover on & off Information button

  • Hover on & off "watch now" button in modal

Rules

  • Hover on image:
    • White circle with “i” fades in over time on left corner of image

  • Hover on Information button:
    • Translucent green rectangle swipes in from left to overlay the image
    • "i" bounces down slightly into rectangle to show movement
    • Extra info text & "watch now" button appear

  • Hover on "watch now button":
    • Color fades to a slightly darker color over time

  • Hover off "watch now button":
    • Color fades back to its original color over time

  • Hover off Information button:
    • Translucent green rectangle goes away
    • "i" bounces back up to its original place
    • Extra info text & "watch now" button disappear

  • Hover off image:
    • White circle with “i” on left corner of image fades out over time
