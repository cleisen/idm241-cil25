IDM 241 ALPHA
Microinteraction:
How it Works:

Triggers

  • Hover on image of TV show on "Browse" page

  • Hover off image of TV show on "Browse" page

Rules

  • Hover on:
    - Mouse pointer turns to a finger
    - Image zooms in about 1.05x over time
    - White gradient outer glow fades in behind image
    - White border appears around image

  • Hover off:
    - Finger pointer turns back to a mouse
    - Image zooms out about -1.05x over time
    - White gradient outer glow behind image disappears instantly
    - White border around image disappears instantly

Feedback

  • Hover on:
    - Mouse pointer turns to a finger
    - Image zooms in about 1.05x over time
    - White gradient outer glow fades in behind image
    - White border appears around image

  • Hover off:
    - Finger pointer turns back to a mouse
    - Image zooms out about -1.05x over time
    - White gradient outer glow behind image disappears instantly
    - White border around image disappears instantly
